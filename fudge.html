Fudge

Fudge is also a Mini-lop, his colouring is called ‘Opal’ and each strand of his fur is striped brown and black. He is a very shy rabbit, who likes to quietly munch on his straw, if he is scared, he hides in his cage. Fudge’s favourite food is Kale, whenever he eats it, his whole body twitches because he is enjoying it so much!
