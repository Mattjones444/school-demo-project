Marshmallow

Marshmallow is also a Mini-lop rabbit, his colouring is ‘Tortoise-shell Split’, his brother is Fudge. He is a fun and playful boy, who loves attention and being stroked on his back. Marshmallow loves to groom Coco and they are best friends.
